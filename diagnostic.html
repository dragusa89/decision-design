The Diagnostic

Clarity about the problem
is the first act
of solving it.

Most designers know something is wrong with how their decisions are being handled. Very few have seen it clearly enough to know where to start.

№ 02 — Diagnostic Duration 60–90 min Format 1:1
A Filter, Not a Funnel

One conversation.
Two clear definitions.

What It Is

  • A structured 60–90 minute conversation.
  • We map your decision environment.
  • Where are you losing control?
  • Which conversations are bypassing you?
  • What frame is being set before you arrive?

You leave with a clear picture of your situation and a frame for how to approach it.

What It Isn't

  • Not a free intro call.
  • Not a UX audit.
  • Not a coaching session.
  • Not a pitch for further services.

The Diagnostic stands on its own.
It's not a funnel — it's a filter.

Investment

After the Session

Three doors.
You choose.

i.

You run with it yourself.

You take the clarity you've gained and apply it. Many people do exactly this — and that's the point.

ii.

A defined engagement.

We continue with a specific scope — a pitch, a negotiation, a relationship. Fixed range.

iii.

An ongoing retainer.

By invitation only. Discussed only if it's a clear fit, and never on the day of the Diagnostic.

Either way, the Diagnostic stands on its own. You won't be asked to buy anything in the room.
