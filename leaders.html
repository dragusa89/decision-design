The Organizational Dynamic

The decision happens
before the meeting.

Corporate design leaders face a specific version of the problem: the decision happens before the meeting.

By the time you're presenting your team's work to the executive layer, someone has already framed the conversation — usually in terms that don't include design authority.

The solution isn't better presentation skills.
The solution is being part of the decision architecture before the meeting is scheduled.
