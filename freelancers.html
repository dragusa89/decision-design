Where Control Breaks Down

The moment before
the number

There's one moment that determines everything in a freelance client relationship: the moment they ask what this is going to cost.

Everything before that moment — the way you framed the conversation, the assumptions they arrived with, the authority they gave you — determines whether you have to defend the number or whether it's already obvious.

Most freelancers manage the number.
Decision Design manages the moment before the number.
