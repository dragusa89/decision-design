Plain Answers

Asked often.
Answered the same way.

Can the fee be invoiced to a company?+

Yes. Hungarian VAT (ÁFA) applies where required. EU reverse-charge for B2B clients with a valid VAT ID.

Do you offer a free intro call?+

No. The Diagnostic is the first conversation. That's deliberate — it removes the sales theatre and lets us do real work the first time we meet.

What if it isn't a fit after the Diagnostic?+

That's a normal outcome — and the design of the practice. The Diagnostic stands on its own. You leave with the frame regardless.

Cancellations and rescheduling?+

Reschedule freely up to 48 hours before the slot. Inside 48 hours, the slot is forfeit — the time is held for you and isn't easy to fill.
